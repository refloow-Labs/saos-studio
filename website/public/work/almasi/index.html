The Almasi Story

Almasi means diamond.

Nestled on the vibrant Eleftheriou Venizelou street, Almasi Luxury Suites is a boutique guesthouse built on a simple idea: that the best travel memories come from places that feel personal, not polished to anonymity.

Every suite is designed with quiet luxury in mind — warm textures, considered details, and the kind of comfort that makes you forget you're not home. The Thracian Sea breeze, the golden light of northeastern Greece, and a host who genuinely cares complete the picture.

The Almasi Experience

More than a stay.

Small things, done with intention. This is what sets a guesthouse apart from a hotel.

01

Homemade Breakfast

Fresh pastries, local Thracian honey, seasonal fruit, and strong Greek coffee — served each morning in the dining room or on the terrace.

02

Personal Concierge

Restaurant bookings, hidden beaches, the best sunset spot — your host knows Alexandroupoli like a local because they are one.

03

Evening Wine Hour

A complimentary glass of Thracian wine on the terrace as the sun dips behind the harbour. The perfect end to a day by the sea.

04

Curated City Guides

Hand-drawn maps and honest recommendations — the taverna the tourists miss, the morning market route, the quietest beach.

05

Premium Amenities

High-speed WiFi, premium toiletries, luxury linens, climate control, and soundproofed rooms for an undisturbed rest.

06

Flexible Check-in

Early arrivals, late departures — we work around your schedule, not ours. Travel should feel effortless.
