Privacy Policy

SAOS Studio ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and address when you inquire or engage our services.
  • Project Content: Text, images, logos, and brand materials you provide for your website.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website (via analytics).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies only. No third-party tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To communicate with you about your project
  • To deliver the services you have requested
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with:

  • Hosting providers (to deploy your website)
  • Email service providers (to communicate with you)
  • Legal authorities (if required by law)

4. Data Retention

We retain your project files for 12 months after project completion in case you need updates or revisions. After this period, files are securely deleted unless you request earlier removal.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@saos.studio.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage, secure file transfer, and access controls.

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
