## 1. Brand Positioning

**Positioning Statement:**
> For small businesses that need a professional online presence, SAOS Studio is an AI-accelerated web design partner that delivers agency-quality websites in under a week for under €500. Unlike traditional agencies that charge €3,000+ and take 6–8 weeks, we combine AI speed with human refinement to ship beautiful, conversion-focused sites at a fraction of the cost and time.

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Price:** Under €500 (vs. €3K–€10K market rate)
- **Speed:** 48 hours to 7 days (vs. 4–8 weeks)
- **Quality:** Hand-finished by designers; no templates
- **Simplicity:** One brief call. No bloated process.

## 2. Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Where to Find Them | Pain Points |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Local Service Businesses** | Restaurants, cafés, salons, clinics, gyms, auto shops | Google Maps, Yelp, local directories | No website or 2005-era site; losing customers to competitors |
| **Solo Professionals** | Lawyers, accountants, architects, photographers, consultants | LinkedIn, Instagram, local business groups | Need credibility; DIY builders look amateur |
| **Early-Stage Startups** | Pre-seed / bootstrapped SaaS, apps, local brands | IndieHackers, Product Hunt, Twitter/X | Need landing pages fast to validate; no budget for agencies |
| **Trades & Construction** | Plumbers, electricians, builders, painters | Google Maps, local Facebook groups | Rely entirely on word-of-mouth; no digital footprint |

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
- Business owner aged 30–55
- Has been operating 1–5 years
- Currently has no website OR a very old/poor one
- Active on at least one social channel
- Speaks English or local language (start with your market)
- Decision-maker (owner, not a marketing manager with a budget)

## 4. Acquisition Channels

### A. Direct Outreach (Highest ROI — Start Here)
**Method:** Scrape Google Maps for businesses with bad/no websites → Generate AI mockup → Send personalized cold email with preview link.
- **Volume:** 50 outreach emails/day
- **Target conversion:** 2–5% reply rate, 10–20% of replies become meetings, 50% of meetings close
- **Projected:** 50 emails/day → 1–2 deals/week → €2K–€4K/month from outreach alone

### B. Local SEO & Google Business Profile
- Create SAOS Studio Google Business Profile
- Post weekly updates ("Website delivered for [Client] in [City]")
- Ask every client for a Google review
- Target keywords: "web design [city]", "affordable website [city]", "AI web design [country]"

### C. Social Proof Flywheel (Instagram / LinkedIn)
- Post before/after screenshots of client websites
- Share "Website teardowns" — show a bad site and your redesign
- Behind-the-scenes of AI + human workflow
- Target: 1 post per day, 3 stories per day
- Hashtags: #webdesign #smallbusiness #branding #ai #websitedesign

### D. Referral Program
- Every client gets a €100 credit for each referral that closes
- Frame it as: "Know another business that needs this?"

### E. Partnerships
- Partner with business accountants, business coaches, and marketing consultants
- Offer them 15% commission on closed deals
- They have direct access to our ICP

### F. Directory Listings
- Clutch.co, GoodFirms, Sortlist, Bark, Fiverr Pro, Upwork (selective)
- These take time but build long-term inbound

### G. Content Marketing (Month 2+)
- Blog posts: "How much should a small business website cost?"
- Comparison posts: "Wix vs. SAOS Studio"
- YouTube/TikTok short: "I redesigned a local café website in 3 hours"

## 7. Brand Voice & Messaging

**Tone:** Confident but warm. Expert but approachable. Editorial but clear.

**Do:**
- Use elegant, refined language
- Show, don't tell (visuals > adjectives)
- Be direct about pricing
- Speak to outcomes ("more customers", "better credibility")

**Don't:**
- Use buzzwords like "synergy", "disrupt", "leverage"
- Sound like a cheap freelancer
- Over-explain the AI (it’s a tool, not the product)
- Apologize for the price being low

**Message Hierarchy:**
1. **Headline:** Beautiful websites under €500
2. **Sub:** Agency quality. Delivered in days.
3. **Proof:** Portfolio + process + testimonials
4. **CTA:** Email us / Book a call
