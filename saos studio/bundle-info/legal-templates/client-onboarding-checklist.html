Client Onboarding Checklist

Client: Project: Date:

Pre-Project
Discovery call completed — Goals, audience, competitors discussed
Proposal sent & approved — Scope, price, timeline confirmed in writing
Service Agreement signed — Both parties have signed contract
Scope of Work attached — Specific pages and features documented
Deposit received (50%) — Invoice paid, confirmed in bank
Content Collection
Logo files received — PNG, SVG, or AI format
Brand guidelines / colors — Hex codes, fonts, mood notes
All page copy received — Final text for every page
Images / photos received — High-res, properly licensed
Contact details confirmed — Address, phone, email, hours, social links
Domain & hosting info — Registrar login or preferred host
Google Business Profile link — For schema and local SEO
Production
AI draft generated — Initial design created and reviewed internally
Client draft sent — Preview link shared for feedback
Revision round 1 complete — Client feedback incorporated
Revision round 2 complete — Final polish applied
Mobile testing passed — iPhone + Android checked
PageSpeed score checked — Target 90+ on mobile & desktop
SEO basics configured — Meta tags, alt text, headings, schema
Launch
Final approval received — Client confirms they are satisfied
Final invoice sent & paid — 50% balance cleared
Domain connected — DNS configured, SSL active
Site deployed live — Public and indexable
Analytics verified — GA or Plausible receiving data
Handover email sent — Login info, tips, next steps
Review requested — Google Business, Trustpilot, or testimonial
Case study documented — Screenshots, metrics, quote captured
