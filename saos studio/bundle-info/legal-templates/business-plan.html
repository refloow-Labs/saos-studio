Executive Summary

Mission: To democratize beautiful web design by combining artificial intelligence with human creative direction — delivering professional websites in under a week for under €500.

The Opportunity

Millions of small businesses operate with outdated, broken, or nonexistent websites. Traditional agencies charge €3,000–€10,000 and require 6–8 week timelines. DIY builders produce generic results that fail to build trust. There is a massive gap for fast, affordable, high-quality web design.

The Solution

SAOS Studio uses AI to accelerate the design and coding process by 80%, then applies senior human designers to refine the final 20% — spacing, typography, animation, and brand voice. The result is a website that looks and performs like a €10,000 agency build, delivered in 2–7 days.

Marketing & Sales Strategy

Primary Channel: Direct Outreach

Our "Draft First, Sell Second" system identifies businesses with poor/no websites via Google Maps, generates a custom AI homepage preview for their brand, and sends a personalized email with a live preview link. This turns cold outreach into a warm demonstration.

Target: 50 emails/day → 2–5% reply rate → 10–20% meeting conversion → 50% close rate = 1–2 deals/week from outbound alone.

Secondary Channels

  • Local SEO: Google Business Profile, local keywords, reviews
  • Social Proof Flywheel: Instagram/LinkedIn before/after posts
  • Referral Program: €100 credit per successful referral
  • Partnerships: Accountants, coaches, business consultants (15% commission)
  • Content: Blog posts comparing agency vs. AI design costs

Sales Process

  1. Discovery Call (15 min): Understand goals, content, timeline
  2. Proposal: One-page scope + price confirmation via email
  3. Deposit: 50% upfront to begin
  4. Draft Delivery: AI-generated draft within 24–48 hours
  5. Revision Round: Client feedback, designer refinement
  6. Final Payment: 50% on approval
  7. Launch: Deploy, connect domain, handover
