The House of Armadoro

Luxury that lives in the water.

Born in Athens and made for Mediterranean life, Galleria Armadoro is fine jewellery built for the way you actually live — sea swims, late lunches, suncream and all. Gold vermeil, sterling silver and brass, finished to last.

Every piece is waterproof and made to be layered, stacked and worn on repeat. No taking off before the swim. No precious little ceremony. Just jewellery you forget you're wearing — until someone asks where it's from.
