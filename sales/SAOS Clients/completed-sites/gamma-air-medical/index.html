Core Services
Our Mission

Saving Lives Through
Safe Medical Transport

Gamma Air Medical E.P.E. was founded with a single, unwavering purpose: the safe transportation of patients who are suffering. Every operation we conduct, every route we plan, and every team member we deploy serves this critical mission.

From the moment of first contact to final delivery at the receiving facility, we provide comprehensive bedside-to-bedside service, ensuring continuity of care at every stage of the journey.

"Bedside to bedside — because every moment matters when a life is at stake."
What We Do

Comprehensive Medical
Aviation Services

Nine specialized service lines covering every aspect of medical transport, aviation consultancy, and healthcare logistics.

01

Air Ambulance Flights

Patient medical transfer via fixed and rotary wing aircraft, equipped with advanced life-support systems and staffed by specialist medical crews.

02

Operations Consultancy

Expert aviation and medical interior consulting for air medical operations, aircraft configuration, and regulatory compliance.

03

Medical Escort

Qualified medical professionals accompanying patients on scheduled commercial flights for safe, monitored travel.

04

Car Ambulance Services

Ground medical transfer with fully equipped ambulance vehicles, ensuring seamless bedside-to-aircraft-to-bedside transport chains.

05

Medical Clearance

"Fit to Fly" medical assessments, comprehensive medical reports, and clearance documentation for patient air travel.

06

Expenses Control

Health expenses control and containment services to manage and optimize the financial aspects of medical transport and care.

07

Congress Organization

Planning and executing medical and aviation workshops, congresses, and professional development events for the industry.

08

First Aid Courses

Certified first aid training programs designed specifically for airline crew, aviation personnel, and medical transport teams.

09

Medical Kits

Supply of professionally curated first aid medical kits and precaution kits meeting international aviation and medical standards.
