Our Story

A Different Type
of Irish Pub

We blend the best of the old with the best of the new. Dark wood, long bar and warm comfortable environment evoke images of the great old pubs of Ireland whereas our style of operating connects with contemporary pubs of modern Ireland.

“At The James Joyce it’s a place where there are no strangers, only friends you haven’t met.”

Since opening our doors on October 12, 2007, we have brought the spirit of Dublin to the heart of Athens. Nestled in the vibrant Monastiraki district, we offer an authentic Irish experience — from our perfectly poured pints to our hearty pub classics.
