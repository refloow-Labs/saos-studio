Welcome to Destino Blue

A contemporary retreat on Corfu's emerald coast

Nestled in the heart of Sidari, Destino Blue Hotel & Spa is a contemporary and stylish getaway on Corfu's most captivating coastline. Just a short walk from the golden sands of Sidari Beach, the legendary Canal D'Amour, and the secluded Apotripiti Beach.

Every detail has been crafted to create an atmosphere of relaxed luxury -- from our swim-up suites overlooking glistening pools to our world-class spa and Mediterranean dining experiences.

"Arrive as a guest, leave as a friend."

Curated Experiences

Bars

Two distinctive bars to match your mood -- an elegant cocktail bar for sunset aperitivos and a vibrant pool bar for sun-soaked refreshments throughout the day.
