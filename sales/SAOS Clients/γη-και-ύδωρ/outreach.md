Δημοκρατίας 363, the restaurant boasts a 4.8-star Google rating with 142 reviews — strong social proof for a regional restaurant. The name itself ("earth and water" in Ancient Greek) suggests a philosophy rooted in natural, local ingredients: produce from the Thracian hinterland and seafood from the Aegean coast. The restaurant has no current website, representing a clear opportunity for a digital presence that would extend their already-strong reputation to a wider audience — tourists, travelers along the Egnatia Odos corridor, and younger local diners who research online before booking.

**Opportunity:** First website, high-intent audience (Alexandroupoli is a port city and university town), strong existing reviews to leverage, distinct brand concept ("earth & water") that translates naturally into visual storytelling.
